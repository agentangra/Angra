Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Angra Etsy Draft Tools is a private utility used by the Etsy shop owner to create draft listings from prepared digital artwork files. The tool is not a public service and is used only with the shop owner’s authorization.

Information accessed

With the shop owner’s OAuth approval, the tool may access limited Etsy account, shop, and listing information needed to create and verify draft listings. This may include shop identifiers, listing titles, descriptions, images, tags, materials, and digital listing files.

How information is used

Information is used only to prepare Etsy draft listings requested by the shop owner. The tool does not sell, rent, or share Etsy data with third parties.

Publishing control

The tool is designed to create draft listings only. The shop owner reviews drafts in Etsy and manually chooses whether to publish them.

Token storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the owner-controlled server or workstation used for the automation. Tokens are not intentionally transmitted to third parties except Etsy as required for authenticated API access.

Data retention

Draft listing manifests and local logs may be kept by the shop owner to verify what was created. The shop owner may delete local tokens, logs, and generated files at any time.

Contact

This is a private shop-owner tool. For questions, contact the Etsy shop owner directly.
